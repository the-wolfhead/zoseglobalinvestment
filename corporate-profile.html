Corporate Profile

Structured Growth. Diversified Operations. Sustainable Impact.

Company Overview

Zose Global Investment LTD is a diversified Nigerian investment and operations company engaged in healthcare, manufacturing, retail trade, agriculture, food distribution, real estate development, and strategic consultancy services. The Company operates as a multi-sector enterprise with structured divisions designed to drive efficiency, sustainability, and economic growth.

Our Vision

To become a leading diversified investment enterprise in Nigeria, delivering sustainable value across multiple industries.

Our Mission

To build structured, innovative, and profitable business operations that contribute meaningfully to national development and global partnerships.

Core Values

Sustainability

Long-term economic and environmental responsibility.

High standards in service delivery and operational execution.

Business Structure

Zose Global Investment LTD operates through structured business units:

  • Manufacturing & Production Division
  • Retail & Supermarket Operations
  • Food & Agricultural Distribution
  • Real Estate Development
  • Agriculture & Consultancy Services

Leadership Statement

Our leadership team is committed to building a structured, diversified enterprise capable of competing both locally and internationally. We focus on governance, compliance, operational efficiency, and strategic expansion across priority sectors in Nigeria.
