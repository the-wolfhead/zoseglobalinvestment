About Zose Global Investment LTD

Incorporated in Nigeria, Zose Global Investment LTD operates as a diversified investment and operations company with interests spanning healthcare services, manufacturing and production, retail trade, food and agricultural distribution, real estate development, and consultancy services. The Company is committed to contributing meaningfully to national economic growth through structured and sustainable business operations.
