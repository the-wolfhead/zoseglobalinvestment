About Zose Global Investment LTD

Corporate Overview

Zose Global Investment LTD is a duly incorporated Nigerian company established to operate as a diversified investment and operations enterprise. The Company engages in multi-sector business activities including healthcare services, manufacturing and production, retail trade, food and agricultural distribution, real estate transactions, and consultancy services.

The Company is structured to operate through clearly defined business divisions, each aligned with national development priorities and long-term economic sustainability.

Vision

To be a leading diversified Nigerian enterprise delivering value across strategic sectors of the economy.

Mission

To develop and manage sustainable business operations across healthcare, manufacturing, retail, agriculture, food distribution, real estate and consultancy through innovation, operational excellence and integrity.

Core Values

Integrity

Commitment to ethical business conduct and transparency.

Excellence

Delivery of high-quality products and services across all divisions.

Innovation

Adoption of modern technologies and efficient operational systems.

Sustainability

Responsible business practices contributing to long-term economic growth.

Corporate Governance

Zose Global Investment LTD maintains a structured governance framework designed to ensure accountability, compliance with regulatory requirements, and prudent management of corporate resources.

The Company operates in accordance with applicable Nigerian laws and industry standards relevant to its respective sectors.

Strategic Objectives

  • Expansion of diversified business operations across Nigeria.
  • Development of technology-driven platforms and infrastructure.
  • Strengthening distribution and retail networks.
  • Investment in agricultural and manufacturing capabilities.
  • Long-term sustainable value creation for stakeholders.
